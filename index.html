About the mission

Bridging the learning gap with simple, reliable technology.

Many students in rural areas have talent but limited access to structured digital education, trusted guidance, and affordable academic support. Sharusuri EdTech aims to bring quality learning closer to those families.

Admissions

A simple enrollment journey for students and parents.

Keep this process visible so families know exactly what happens after they submit interest.

Start Admission Enquiry
  1. Submit interest Student or parent sends basic details through the enquiry form.
  2. Counselling call Team discusses class level, learning goals, fees, and available batches.
  3. Diagnostic session Optional assessment helps recommend the right program and pace.
  4. Batch confirmation Admission is confirmed after documents and payment details are completed.

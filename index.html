Our 3-Phase Scalability Roadmap

📍 Phase 1: Village Pilot

Launching in 5–10 local villages. Proving student engagement using offline-ready, hyper-compressed learning modules running on low-end smartphones.

🏢 Phase 2: City Scale

Expanding to tier-2 and tier-3 regional towns. Demonstrating strong operational unit economics, automated IT backend scaling, and local user traction.

🇮🇳 Phase 3: Nationwide

Partnering with tier-1 Venture Capital funds to deploy multi-lingual, personalized learning systems to millions of homes across India.

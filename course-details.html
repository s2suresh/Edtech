01

Basic Foundation

For early learners who need confidence in learning basics.

This course focuses on reading, writing, number sense, discipline, and guided practice so students can build a strong academic base.

  • Basic language and number practice
  • Learning habits and confidence building
  • Parent-friendly progress guidance
02

Classes 1-3 Fundamentals

For primary students who need strong fundamentals.

This track supports foundational Maths, language practice, guided activities, and simple study routines for young students.

  • Basic Maths and language support
  • Activity-based learning practice
  • Revision and doubt clearing
03

Classes 4-5 Competitive

For Navodaya, Morarji Desai, and other entrance preparation.

This program helps students prepare for competitive entrance-style exams through reasoning, Maths, language, practice questions, and exam confidence.

  • Navodaya preparation support
  • Morarji Desai preparation support
  • Other competitive entrance practice
04

Classes 6-10 Maths

For school Maths concept clarity and exam improvement.

This track focuses on chapter-wise concept clarity, problem solving, regular practice, and doubt clearing for Classes 6-10 students.

  • Chapter-wise Maths support
  • Problem-solving practice
  • Exam-focused revision planning
05

+1 and +2 Physics & Maths

For higher secondary subject support and exam readiness.

This program supports +1 and +2 students with Physics and Mathematics concepts, numerical practice, assignments, and exam preparation planning.

  • Physics concept and numerical practice
  • Mathematics problem-solving support
  • Higher secondary exam preparation

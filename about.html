FAQ

Questions families usually ask.

Is this fully online or offline?

The recommended model is hybrid: digital learning content with local guidance and follow-up support.

Can parents ask questions before admission?

Yes. Families can contact through phone, WhatsApp, email, or the admission enquiry form.

Will more pages and services be added later?

Yes. Teacher profiles, gallery, downloads, calendar, and login features can be added as the project grows.

Recommended flow

From enquiry to payment confirmation.

This keeps student data, course choice, and payment proof together before classes start.

01Submit enquiry

Fill the website Admission Enquiry form with student, course, contact, and location details.

02Receive email PDF

The system stores the response in Google Sheet and sends a PDF receipt to the submitted email address.

03Confirm course and fee

Admission team confirms class level, batch timing, fee amount, and start date.

04Pay by UPI QR

Student or parent pays using the official QR code after the amount is confirmed.

05Share proof on WhatsApp

Send payment screenshot and enquiry reference ID for manual confirmation.
